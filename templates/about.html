ABOUT CALMDOWN

Founded in June 2022, CalmDown is an interactive website incorporated with a stress reduction recommmendation system. Our aim is to provide our clients with stress reduction methods without medications and help them live their lives to the fullest, in a relaxed, happy, healthy, and stress free way. These methods are provided based on individual stress levels calculated with the help of a standard scale known as Percieved Stress Scale. We also provide related articles, tools to help reduce stress.

CalmDown is a project completed by 4 ambitious engineering students with the hope of helping those stressed minds.

WHY CALMDOWN ?

CalmDown uses carefully curated questions designed after working with psychologists to detect the degree of stress to present you with tailored recommendations that best suit your stress level.

To deliver the finest recommendations, the most latest and powerful machine learning models are combined with feedbacks from hundreds of people all around the world.
